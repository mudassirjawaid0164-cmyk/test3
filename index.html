About the Practice

Personalized medicine with continuity, clarity, and trust.

Mustafa Rahim MD Inc provides adult internal medicine care in Beckley, West Virginia, with a focus on compassionate treatment, same-day access, diagnostic support, and long-term patient relationships.
Dr. Mustafa Rahim is Associate Professor of Medicine at WVU School of Medicine. He is Board Certified in Internal Medicine, a Certified Specialist in Clinical Hypertension, and a Civil Surgeon for U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS).

Services

Care designed around real patient needs.

Adult Internal Medicine

Comprehensive adult care, chronic disease management, geriatrics, and evaluation of illnesses affecting multiple body systems.

Hospital & Admitting Coordination

Office evaluation with timely coordination for hospital care, direct admitting support when appropriate, and continuity across care settings.

Preventive & Screening Services

Wellness visits, preventive care, diabetes screening, hypertension screening, lipid management, and routine health maintenance.

On-Site Testing & Exams

On-site ancillary testing including labs, EKG, pulmonary function testing, ultrasound services, Holter monitoring, echocardiogram support, physicals, and USCIS immigration exams.
